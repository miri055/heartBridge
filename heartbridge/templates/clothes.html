Small enterprises

Small businesses often face challenges with aging or slightly damaged stock that can't be sold traditionally. But what if this "waste" could become someone's treasure, offering warmth, dignity, and a chance for a better life? Heartbridge provides a unique opportunity for small enterprises to transform their old and damaged stock into impactful donations that empower underserved communities.

Household

Every household has clothes that no longer spark joy – items tucked away in drawers, gathering dust instead of warming someone in need. Heartbridge bridges this gap, connecting the abundance in your closet with the essential needs of communities facing clothing insecurity. Donating your gently used clothes isn't just decluttering, it's an act of kindness with a ripple effect.
